Target Clients

This project is aimed at companies with 10 to 200 employees in the tourism, commerce, and logistics sectors, located in Andorra, Girona, and Barcelona. However, we will never turn away anyone who requests our services.

Parts of Our Services

  • Premium Support: phone assistance and on-site technicians in under 2 hours.
  • Consulting: help with migrating old data, employee training, and custom adaptations.
